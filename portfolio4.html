Hazelbrook House, Bunratty County Clare

Hazelbrook House was originally built in 1898 and is located in Bunratty Folk Park, County Clare. The tourism board of Shannon Development conracted us to renovate this two storey farmhouse required complete refurbishment of both storeys and a complete refurbishment of the building's exterior. This project required to renovate and refurbish the building to replicate the interior and exterior as it was in 1898.

Project Details

  • Projects Value: €1m
  • Project Duration: 8 months
  • Design Team: John Gaynor
    Barry Sheppard and Partners
