John Gaynor Construction
Your Performance Driven Partner

From multi-national firms to private clients, and from local authorities to historical buildings – JGC is the strength behind the building construction industry in Ireland’s Mid West. And we’re ready to bring our skill, professionalism and unquestioned reputation to your project..

JGC is conveniently based in Ennis, Clare and is a performance-driven company specialising in contract work as well as being a competitive force in the tender market.

The foundation of our business is our simple but vital combination of cost-effectiveness, quality, reliability and timely delivery – assured with every project we undertake.
